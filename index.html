About the Artist

Drawn to the fleeting, the luminous, the in-between.

From childhood, Danjue Li found in drawing and painting a rare stillness — a quiet focus that the rest of the world couldn't offer. Art was always a dream, though life took her first into software engineering.

In recent years, she returned to that dream with full devotion, studying watercolor under the influence of renowned artist Xiaochang Zhang. Her practice has since become an exploration of what she loves most about the medium: light.

Based in the Bay Area, Danjue is drawn to the way light transforms ordinary scenes — revealing subtle beauty in the everyday. Through watercolor, she seeks to capture quiet, luminous moments that invite the viewer to slow down and simply observe.
